Our Story

Team Peregrine Warriors was founded with the mission to drive innovation through teamwork, resilience, and creativity.

The Rise of the Peregrine Warriors It all started with four classmates dreaming of joining F1 in Schools. But then Advaith sparked a bold idea

“Let’s join 4x4. We get to drive the car!”

That changed everything. We became the Peregrine Warriors — named after the fastest bird on Earth, united by our motto:

Precision, Power, Perseverance.

Two skilled students from the year above joined us, and together we built something greater than just a car — we built a team. Each member brought new strength, pushing the project higher. From design to driving, branding to engineering, every decision was driven by passion. We’re not just here to compete — We’re here to conquer terrain, soar above challenges, and leave a legacy.
